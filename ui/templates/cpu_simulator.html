Single Cycle

One instruction every clock tick — CPI = 1, the fewest cycles possible. But that one tick must do everything (fetch→decode→ALU→memory→write‑back), so its clock is the slowest of any model. Fewest cycles ≠ fastest. Switch to Configurable to dial cycles per instruction and watch total time.

Cycles each instruction class takes. Spreading work over more cycles shrinks the clock period — watch cycles vs. time below.
